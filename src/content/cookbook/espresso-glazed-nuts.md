## Ingredients

- [ ] 1 cup whole almonds
- [ ] 1 cup whole pecans
- [ ] 1 cup whole walnuts
- [ ] 1 espresso shot (~2.5 oz)
- [ ] 1/2 cup maple syrup
- [ ] 1/2 TBSP pure vanilla extract
- [ ] 1/8 tsp cinnamon
- [ ] 2 TBSP coconut oil

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 300°F. Line a rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper or a non-stick mat.
2. Whisk together espresso, maple syrup, vanilla, cinnamon, and coconut oil.
3. Toss nuts in the glaze and let rest for 10 minutes.
4. Using a slotted spoon, transfer nuts to the prepared pan in a single layer. Drizzle remaining glaze on top.
5. Bake for 10 minutes, then stir gently and return to oven for another 7-10 minutes until toasted.
6. Cool completely before serving. Store in an airtight container.
