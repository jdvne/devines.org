## Ingredients

### Salad
- [ ] 8 cups broccoli florets, cut into bite-sized pieces
- [ ] 1/3 cup red onion, diced
- [ ] 1/2 cup dried cranberries
- [ ] 1/4 cup sunflower seeds
- [ ] 1/2 cup bacon bits

### Dressing
- [ ] 1 cup mayonnaise
- [ ] 3 TBSP apple cider vinegar
- [ ] 2 TBSP granulated sugar
- [ ] Salt and black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Whisk together mayo, vinegar, sugar, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl. Set aside.
2. In a large bowl, combine broccoli, red onion, cranberries, sunflower seeds, and bacon bits.
3. Pour dressing over the salad and toss thoroughly to coat.
4. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
